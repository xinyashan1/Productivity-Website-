About

Our challenge was to create a solution where people don't loose track of their organization flow and stay sane. During times like this it is crucial to make sure that there is still room left to complete your tasks. In addition to that our goal is to design a technical solution so that people can be apart of the digital transformation.

The main goal

To create a application that helps boost productivity while de-stressing 10 times faster! Our goal is to make sure that people believe in this product and so we decided to create a Self care club as well.

Aditional features

Even though the digitla life has taken over most of our routine that doesn't mean we are like that! Our additional features include some products you can keep! For example a smart water bottle, motivational plush, and subscription box goodie that can be delivered faster than speed of light.

    Things we include

    Design

    The logo, calendar, and wireframes were designed by Joseph! So we've brainstormed a lot of different themes and we concluded blue would be the main color. In addition according to marketing psychology blue is color that makes people feel calm. In addition to our brainstorming sessions, we've concluded that there are colors that make people feel calmer and relax and so because this app is designed to delete the stress the colors being decided have been thought out carefully. On top of that sometimes there are those colors that make people more anxious. For example, red because it reminds of people of the errors they've made when they get back that homework assignment. Therefore as a result we try to stay away from colors that trigger those feelings.

    Plaforms

    For this project, some of the things that will be presented are Pitch Presentation, UI/UX design that focuses on additional features, graphic designs, and research that discusses self-care, psychology, and the importance of being healthy both mentally and physically. Our main focus is to increase productivity and focus on getting things done with the least amount of distraction and stress. Even though the virtual life may not be everyone's cup of tea we sincerely hope that at least some of our ideas can help out even if it is just a tiny bit. Furthermore, this product's main objective is to assure that people are forming better habits so they are the best version of themselves. As for versions we've decided it is best to include versions for slack, microsoft office and google extension so that it be available for a wide range of people in different platform communities.

    Digital World

    Since life, as we know it has changed and gone digital, is the main source of staying in communication for most people we figure that creating an application would be the best product to get produced during a time like this. First, we were given three different challenges to choose from. We collectively decided it is best to develop an idea where we would design something user-friendly, affordable, and relatable for everyone. Based on our research and brainstorming sessions we've concluded that most people dislike online classes and so we wanted to turn that problem into a solution for those who do not cope well with the remote life. In the meantime, we have also asked for a lot of feedback on how we are doing and some mentors mentioned they are already used to working from home and the majority suggest a system where color-coded calendars would help them out a lot.
